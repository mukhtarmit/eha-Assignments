Jollof Rice

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 minutes
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Rice 4 cups
Tomato Paste 3 tbsp
Onion 1 large
Vegetable Oil 1/2 cup

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes and onions.
  2. Fry tomato paste in oil.
  3. Add blended mixture and seasonings.
  4. Add rice and water.
  5. Cook until rice is soft and flavorful.

Source: All Nigerian Recipes

Pancakes

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Flour 2 cups
Milk 1½ cups
Eggs 2
Sugar 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Pour batter onto a hot pan.
  4. Cook both sides until golden brown.
  5. Serve with syrup or honey.

Source: BBC Good Food

Fruit Salad

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: None
  • Servings: 5

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Apples 2
Bananas 2
Watermelon 2 cups
Grapes 1 cup

Steps

  1. Wash all fruits thoroughly.
  2. Cut fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Mix in a large bowl.
  4. Chill for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve fresh.

Source: Simply Recipes
